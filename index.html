About Elanva

Join Hands, Change the World

Elanva is a global event consulting and production company creating impactful, forward-thinking experiences that connect academia, industry, and innovation. Founded in 2022, we specialize in curating academic conferences, industry summits, exhibitions, and hybrid events that foster collaboration and drive progress.

What sets us apart is our end-to-end approach—from concept and content development to logistics, branding, and post-event engagement. Our flagship NanoSeries conferences, held in global cities like Madrid and Lisbon, showcase our commitment to high-quality, international event production.
